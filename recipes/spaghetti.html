Spaghetti

Home

Description

This spaghetti is the best spaghetti you'll ever
have in your life! It's soooooooo good, you'll never go to
an Italian restaurant again. Get ready to hear Mom say, "We've got Italian
at home!"

Ingredients:

  • spaghetti noodles
  • salt
  • store-bought pasta sauce
  • 1/2 lb ground beef

Preparation:

  1. Place a pot of water over high heat.

  2. Add salt and bring to a boil.

  3. While waiting for water to boil, place skillet over medium heat.

  4. Brown the ground beef, drain grease and season to liking.

  5. Add pasta sauce to meat and simmer.

  6. Once the water is boiling, snap noodles in half and drop into water.

  7. Boil noodles for 8 minutes.

  8. Once noodles are cooked, drain most of the water from the pot.

  9. Serve meat sauce over noodles and enjoy!
