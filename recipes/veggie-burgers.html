Veggie Burgers

Home

Description:

My wife and I absolutely love this recipe! We live the busy life of parents to young children
and we get it! Sometimes you just need a break, a quick and easy meal you can rely on.
Look no further, we've got just what you need!

Ingredients:

  • frozen veggie burger patties
  • sliced cheese
  • brioche bun
  • frozen fries

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.

  2. Arrange single layer of frozen fries on baking sheet.

  3. Bake according to package. (We let ours go longer so they're crispy!)

  4. Approx. 10 minutes into fries bake time, place patties into air fryer;
    cook according to package.

  5. With 2 mins. left to go on patties, place cheese on patties; then begin toasting brioche buns in toaster.

  6. Patties and fries will be ready at the same time. Arrange fries and buns
    on your plate, top with your favorite condiments, put on your favorite show
    and enjoy your new found time together!
