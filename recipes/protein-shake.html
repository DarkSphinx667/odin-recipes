Description

If you're ready for the gainz, you're in the right place! With this simple
yet effective formula, you'll be unstoppable, a living legend!
All that will be left for you to worry about is getting out there and finding your
swolemate!

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop Alani Nu Cookies and Cream protein powder
  • 12 fl. oz. of milk

Preparation

  1. Pour 12 fl. oz. of milk into a shaker bottle.

  2. Add 1 scoop of the protein powder.

  3. Shake until thoroughly combined and enjoy!!
